Online Help:

The Transportation 511 application allows citizens and interested parties to check and monitor the status of the roads across the state. It enables access to timely information regarding accidents, road construction and impedances along the transit system.

Finding Transportation Information

There are four primary ways to find transportation information. You can interact directly with the map clicking roads, or other transportation points of interest. You can use the Search Box to locate an address, road name, work zone, camera, road conditions, wind speed or, road camera. You can also click on any of the transportation points of interest in the 511 information panel or frequently traveled routes in the directions panel. Additional, you can search and print directions of your route.

Map Based Navigation

There are several ways to interact with the map:

1. Tap the road name or transportation point of interest you'd like to see
2. Hold down the shift key and drag a box on the map to zoom in
3. Hold down Ctrl+Shift to zoom out
4. Roll your mouse roller to zoom in/out
5. Drag the zoom slider to zoom in/out

Searching

The search box can be used to find an address, road name, work zone, camera, road conditions, wind speed or, road camera:

1. Enter the address or point of interest into Search
2. Click on the item in the list to locate it on the map

511 Information

You can access 511 Information by tapping on any of the transportation items located in the 511 Information panel. Select the type of information you would like to access, then select the item you are interested in. The map will zoom to the location of item and you'll see a pop up with all the up-to-date information.

Frequently Traveled Routes

You can access Frequently Traveled Routes by tapping on any of the routes listed in the Directions panel. Select the route you're interested in, the map will zoom to the route. You'll be able to see any transportation information available near your selected route. Tap on any of the transportation points of interest to get additional information.

Getting Directions

To get directions you'll need to:

1. Tap the Direction panel
2. Enter the location you wish to depart from
3. Enter the location you wish to arrive at
4. If you wish to add any additional locations to your route then, tap Add Destination and enter the additional location
5. Tap Get Directions
6. Tap Print to get a printed copy of your directions.

Browser Compatibility

The Transportation 511 application is supported on desktops, smartphones, and tablet devices with the following browsers: Microsoft Internet Explorer 8/9/10, Mozilla Firefox, Google Chrome, and Apple Safari.
